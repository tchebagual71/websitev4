About Us

Windansea Inc was founded by Michael Chapon. As a Naval Academy Graduate, I was always interested in the building process. As a commissioned officer I worked with the engineering departments, and was heavily engaged in the renovation projects on the bases in which I was stationed. I eventually left the service to start my own construction company. Now fully certified and licensed to work in all parts of Florida, we have strived to bring the best results to our customers, whether a retail project or an insurance restoration project.

Michael Chapon

President, Windansea Inc
